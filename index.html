About me

I'm just your typical Tampa nerd daydreaming of a galaxy far, far away and attempting to take over the world. My goal in life, both through work and
play, is to challenge popular belief and push the limits of what's possible.
I live to make websites more meaningful and usable. I would probably hack a lemonade stand if it weren't "weird" or "creepy." I'm just that committed. And non-creepy. My roots in sales and community building provide me with a keen understanding
of engagement and interaction design. As a full-stack developer, I geek out over HTML, CSS, Javascript, PHP, APIs, Bootstrap, and all that in between. I'm young, ambitious, and develop sites that are customer-centric. Hailing from the beach bum status in Clearwater, FL, I'm a former teenage life insurance sales agent with some exciting stories. When I'm not building beautiful things,
I'm most likely biking, checking out a live show, playing my bass or honing my MySQL skills. My motto: Try to see life "sunny-side-up." I prefer my yolks runny, and my jokes corny.
